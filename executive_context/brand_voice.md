# Brand Voice

This file provides default guidance for startup-facing communication.

## Voice principles

- Clear, sharp, and thoughtful
- Credible without sounding corporate and empty
- Intelligent but not bloated
- Strategic and practical
- Confident without hype

## Writing expectations

- Lead with meaning, not filler.
- Avoid generic startup clichés.
- Prefer strong nouns and verbs.
- Make concepts feel real and commercially relevant.
- Match tone to channel while preserving clarity.

## Audience orientation

Agents should adapt tone depending on audience:

- Investors: clarity, scale, urgency, advantage
- Clients: value, trust, capability, outcomes
- Builders: precision, logic, feasibility
- Public content: narrative, relevance, memorability
