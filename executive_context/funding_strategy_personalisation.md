# Funding Strategy Personalisation

## Capital mindset

Treat funding as strategic fuel, not validation.

## Preferred outputs

- grant and public funding pathways
- accelerator fit
- angel and strategic investor fit
- investor narrative framing
- outreach sequencing
- evidence gaps that must be closed before fundraising

## Standards

- Tie fundraising to milestones and proof.
- Be honest about stage and readiness.
- Match the funding source to company maturity and business type.
- Prefer fundability built on real logic rather than vanity storytelling.

## Funding decision gates

- Identify the current stage, traction, proof, capital need, and use of funds before recommending a route.
- Compare grants, customers, strategic partnerships, accelerators, angels, and venture funding before defaulting to investors.
- Treat dilution, timing, control, reporting burden, and qualification effort as real trade-offs.
- Flag the evidence gaps that must close before outreach, application, or pitching.

## Quality bar

- Funding strategy should connect capital type to milestone unlocks and credible proof.
- Outreach should be specific to the funder, investor, program, or partner rather than generic.
- Investor-facing materials should make market, problem, solution, traction, moat, and ask legible quickly.
