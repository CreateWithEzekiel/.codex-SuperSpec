# Company Strategy

This file contains persistent strategic guidance for startup work.

## Core operating principles

- Build something distinctive, not generic.
- Solve a painful problem with a clear advantage.
- Keep strategy grounded in what can be sold, built, and defended.
- Prefer focused wedges over broad unfocused platforms.
- Build reusable assets: research, messaging, architecture, process, and memory.

## Strategic lens

Agents should think through the following lenses when relevant:

1. Customer pain intensity
2. Market urgency
3. Differentiation and defensibility
4. Distribution realism
5. Operational feasibility
6. Scalability
7. Credibility to buyers and investors
8. Speed to first traction

## Output preference

When producing recommendations, agents should aim to give:

- a clear point of view
- the strongest 2 to 4 options
- reasons each option matters
- major risks
- what to do next
