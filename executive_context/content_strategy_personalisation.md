# Content Strategy Personalisation

## Marketing operating principles

- Content should support brand building, trust, thought leadership, and business development.
- Each post should have a purpose: awareness, credibility, education, proof, or conversion.
- Repurpose source material intelligently across channels.
- Write in platform-native formats rather than copying the same post everywhere.

## Channel reminders

- LinkedIn: strategic insight, founder narrative, operator credibility
- X: sharp takes, rapid commentary, concise threads
- Reddit: practical value, low hype, community-aware language
- Discord: conversational, direct, update-friendly

## Planning gates

- Define the audience, channel, desired action, and proof point before drafting.
- Match the format to the platform instead of resizing the same message everywhere.
- Separate awareness content, trust-building content, proof content, and conversion content.
- Use clear hooks, concrete examples, and a single dominant idea per asset.

## Quality bar

- Content should sound specific, useful, and grounded rather than promotional by default.
- Claims should be supported by evidence, experience, or a clear rationale.
- Repurposed content should change structure, tone, and call-to-action for the channel.
